2D Game MilseStone 3: Saka On Top!

My game is a two player football game where you try to reach the finals and win. The aim of the game is to touch the ball more times than your opponent, every time a player touches the ball their respective score increments. When the players go to level 2, a referee is spawned in, and they move left and right. If one of the players were to touch the referee then the game ends and the other player automatically wins. Finally in level 3 there will be two referees, making it even harder to touch the ball without being eliminated.
